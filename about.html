About Me

I'm a detail-oriented Full-Stack Developer with a passion for design, harmony, flow, and efficiency. Developing intuitive, beautiful, and high-performing applications drives me to push the envelope of my creativity. It's what keeps me up at night and what wakes me up in the morning.

I started out as a trumpet player with a yearning for user interfaces and high-functioning applications. Naturally, I decided to start programming musical plug-ins and utilities at college. I strive to make web applications as useful and user-friendly as native apps.

I program, design, and develop user-friendly legal forms, sites, and web applications. These applications are used in a variety of mediums and enhance user experience, workflow effectiveness, and database access efficiency.

Languages and Frameworks:

HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, jQuery, Python, SQL [Working on: MongoDB, Express, React.js, Node.js]
